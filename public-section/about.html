About Duhigure mu Miryango

Transforming family development through performance contracts

Program Overview

Duhigure mu Miryango is an innovative community development initiative that brings the performance contract system to the family level. Originating in Kibungo Sector, this program empowers families to take charge of their development journey through structured goal-setting and progress tracking.

The program is built on the principle that sustainable development begins at the family level. By engaging each household in setting and achieving specific development goals, we create a ripple effect that strengthens the entire community.

Program Objectives

Our mission is to empower families through structured development

Goal Setting

Enable families to define clear, achievable development goals aligned with national priorities.

Progress Tracking

Provide tools for monitoring achievements and identifying areas for improvement.

Community Engagement

Foster collaboration between families, volunteers, and administrative authorities.

Scalability Design

Built to expand from Kibungo Sector to nationwide implementation

2023

Phase 1: Kibungo Sector Pilot

Initial launch in Kibungo Sector with 50 pilot families. System testing and refinement.

2024

Phase 2: Bumbogo District Expansion

Expand to all sectors in Bumbogo District. Train Intore volunteers in each sector.

2025

Phase 3: Provincial Rollout

Expand to multiple districts within the province. Enhance system features based on feedback.

2026+

Phase 4: National Implementation

Nationwide rollout with customized features for different regions and communities.

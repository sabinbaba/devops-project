Getting Started

Three Ways to Access the System

Self-Service

Register and use the system independently if you have internet access and basic digital skills.

Intore Assistance

Get help from trained youth volunteers (Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga) in your community.

Sector Office

Visit your sector office for registration and assistance with the system.

Family Registration Process

Step-by-Step Registration

1

Access the System

Visit www.duhigure.rw and click on "System Login" or go directly to the login page.

2

Select Registration Type

Choose "Register New Family" if you're joining for the first time. If you have a Family ID, select "Family Login".

3

Enter Family Information

Provide accurate family details including:

  • Family name and head of household
  • Contact information
  • Location details (province, district, sector)
  • Number of family members
4

Receive Family ID

After successful registration, you'll receive a unique Family ID. Save this for future logins.

Setting Performance Duties

Types of Duties

The system supports two types of performance duties:

Family Duties

Collective goals that involve all family members working together. Examples:

  • Establish a kitchen garden
  • Start a family savings group
  • Improve home sanitation
  • Install rainwater harvesting
Individual Duties

Personal responsibilities for each family member. Examples:

  • Children: Maintain school performance
  • Parents: Attend skills training
  • Youth: Participate in community service
  • All: Learn digital literacy

Creating Effective Duties

Follow the SMART principle when setting duties:

Specific

Measurable

Achievable

Relevant

Time-bound

Progress Tracking

How to Update Progress

Regular Updates

Update your progress at least once a month:

  1. Log in to your family dashboard
  2. Click on the duty you want to update
  3. Enter progress percentage
  4. Add notes or upload photos as evidence
  5. Save your updates

Progress Indicators

Not Started (0%)
In Progress (1-99%)
Completed (100%)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I don't have internet access at home?

You can visit your sector office or meet with an Intore volunteer who can help you access the system using public facilities or their devices.

Q: Can I change my family's performance duties?

Yes, you can modify duties at any time through your dashboard. However, major changes may require approval from your sector coordinator.

Q: How is my data protected?

Your data is protected under Rwanda's data protection laws. Only authorized personnel can access your information, and it's used solely for program improvement.

Q: What happens if I move to another sector?

The system is designed for scalability. Your records can be transferred to your new sector by updating your location in the system settings.

Q: How often should I update progress?

We recommend monthly updates, but you can update as frequently as needed. Regular updates help track progress effectively.
